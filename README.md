## 🧠 The CEH v13 Paradigm Shift

CEH v13 is the first version to fully embed AI-powered attack and defense training. It shifts the focus from manual labor to **AI-augmented hacking**.

* **AI Synergy Engine:** AI acts as an accelerant layer, automating repetitive tasks like reconnaissance and scripting.
* **Human Expertise:** Human operators provide the strategy, ethics, and contextual nuance that AI lacks.
* **Threat Transformation:** Use of AI-powered platforms like Taranis AI for OSINT and ShellGPT for scanning.
* **Defensive Countermeasures:** Implementation of LLM Guards, content classifiers, and explainable AI (XAI).

## 🏗️ Lab Infrastructure & Readiness

To master the **20 domains** and **221 hands-on labs**, the following environment and readiness levels are maintained:

### Technical Stack
* **Primary OS:** Kali Linux (Bare Metal) optimized for security auditing.
* **Virtualization:** VMware Workstation Pro & GNS3 for network orchestration.
* **Containerization:** Docker with MacVLAN isolation for vulnerable application hosting.
* **Optimization:** Custom `zRAM` deployment (75% allocation) to protect SSD health during heavy virtualization.

### Readiness Gauge
* **Networking (TCP/IP, OSI):** 100% — Essential foundation.
* **Linux Systems:** 80% — The primary language of the CEH curriculum.
* **Windows Systems (AD, Registry):** 80% — Critical for 50%+ of hands-on labs.
* **Programming (Python/Bash):** 40% — Helpful for tool manipulation and exploit customization.

## 🎯 Certification Roadmap

The goal is to achieve the **CEH Master** designation by passing the dual-exam architecture:

1.  **ANSI Knowledge Exam:** 125 Multiple-Choice Questions | 4 Hours (Focus: Methodology).
2.  **Practical Exam:** 20 Live Cyber Range Challenges | 6 Hours (Focus: Live Execution).
